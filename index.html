About Me

Background & Interests

I'm a developer based in Michigan, focused on solving real problems with practical software. Much of my work is driven by the needs of people around me — friends, small businesses, and situations where the right tool simply doesn't exist yet. I work primarily on a Mac and enjoy building solutions that bridge different platforms and environments.

One of my current projects is KCI_Pinacle — an application I built to help a friend who needed a reliable way to exchange data between his accounting software and his bank's Positive Pay interface, a fraud-prevention system that protects against check washing. His shop runs on Windows while I develop on Mac, which added an interesting cross-platform dimension to the work.
