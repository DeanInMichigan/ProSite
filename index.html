About Me

Background & Interests

I lead automation and platform engineering teams that turn complex, legacy-heavy environments into scalable, reliable systems. With 25+ years in software engineering and intelligent automation, I specialize in RPA, low-code platforms, and DevOps modernization — blending hands-on technical depth with pragmatic leadership.

My strength sits at the intersection of engineering execution and operational reality. I've built and scaled automation platforms, migrated teams off legacy toolchains, and delivered enterprise systems using fully remote, globally distributed teams. I'm known for stepping into messy environments, creating structure without bureaucracy, and leaving teams stronger than I found them.

Outside of work I'm also a hands-on builder. One of my personal projects is KCI_Pinacle — a Python & Docker application I built to bridge a friend's accounting software with his bank's Positive Pay fraud-prevention interface, solving a real problem with open-source tools at zero licensing cost.
